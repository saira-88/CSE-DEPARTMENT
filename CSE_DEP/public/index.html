About the Department

A decade of academic excellence, innovation and research-driven growth.

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at IIITDM Kurnool was established in 2015 with the launch of B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE). Over the years, the department has expanded significantly in academic intake, infrastructure, research facilities, and industry collaborations.

In 2020, the department introduced B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science (ADS), reflecting the institute’s commitment to emerging technologies. In 2023, postgraduate programs including M.Tech in CSE and M.Tech in Artificial Intelligence & Data Science were launched, strengthening advanced research and specialization.

The curriculum is modeled on ACM recommendations and emphasizes strong foundations in algorithms, systems, artificial intelligence, embedded systems, product design, and hardware–software integration. The department continues to grow through research publications, funded projects, and collaborations with national and international organizations.
